About Us

Our Practice

Orkin Brown and Associates performs independent audit, independent reviews as well as compilation reports for a wide range of clients across various industries.

The practice is registered with the Independent Regulatory Board for Auditors (IRBA).

We have been in business for over 20 years.

Our clients span a breadth of industries, and we are pleased to provide each and every one with the same professional, above-and-beyond service.

Our Team

Our partners, Hilton Orkin and Grant Brown, lead a team of over 30 employees with a wealth and variety of experience.
